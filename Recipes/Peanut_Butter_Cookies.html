Description

Now what is more delicious than something sugary after a long day at work? These cookies sound great at all times of the year, but personally I like them during a cold winter day by a fire with a hot beverage. Yum!

Here's what you'll need to make them:

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Peanut Butter (Crunchy or Smooth)
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Egg

So now that we've seen what ingredients we're working with lets do this and feel happy and relaxed.

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Spray some Pam on a baking sheet
  3. Mix the Peanut Butter, Sugar and egg in a bowl until smooth
  4. Scoop, place on baking tray and bake for 8 mins
  5. Let them rest for 4-5 mins and then enjoy

So there you have it! Yummy, gooey peanut butter cookies. Sit with some hot cocoa with marshmellows on top and just enjoy! Until next time!
