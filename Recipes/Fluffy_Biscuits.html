Fluffy Biscuits

Description

If you're like me and you love biscuits from chicken fast food chains, but after a while it starts making a dent on your wallet. Then you're better off learning how to make them. These take no time at all to prep and just sit and relax while the oven does most of the work!

Lets take a look at what we'll need for a good batch of these delicious fluffiness!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of All Purpose Flour
  • 4 tsp of Sugar
  • 1 Tbs of Baking Powder
  • 1/4 Tsp of baking soda
  • 1 1/4 tsp of Salt
  • 2 Cups of Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Tbs of Melted Butter

Lets get to it!

  1. First preheat the oven to 450 degrees
  2. Wisk dry ingredients (Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, Baking Soda and Salt) until combined
  3. Place 2 cups of Heavy Whipping Cream in microwave for 75 secs.
  4. Add cream to dry ingredients. Stir to combine.
  5. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and scoop dough to the tray
  6. Bake in oven for 12 mins on the upper rack.
  7. Spread the melted butter on them and let rest for 5 mins.

There we go! Delicious biscuits with just a few minutes spent in the kitchen!
