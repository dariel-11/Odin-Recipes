Baked Sweet Potatoes

Description

Today we are going to learn how to bake sweet potatoes with some easy seasoning that only consists of Olive Oil, salt, black pepper and garlic powder. This recipe is so easy that even your kids can make it in no time. So lets get started and put a smile on everyone's face!

Below is what you will need and remember it is all to taste, so by all means please experiment!

Ingredients

  • 4 Sweet Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Optional Ingredients
    • Sour Cream
    • Chives
    • Butter

So what do we do with these ingredients? Easy! Lets begin!

  1. Preheat the oven to 465 degrees.
  2. Chop the chives.
  3. Wash and dry the sweet potatoes in cold water
  4. Cut the potatoes in half.
  5. Pour olive oil on all 8 halves, you can be generous if you want.
  6. Sprinkle in the salt and right after the black pepper.
  7. Sprinkle about a teaspoon worth of Garlic powder between all halves.
  8. When oven is ready put them in a sheet pan, in the oven for 45 mins

And Voila! There you have delicious baked sweet potatoes for lunch or dinner. Don't forget to put as much sour cream as you want and a little bit of the chives to top it off. You can also add bacon bits if you have any lying around.
